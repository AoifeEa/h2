AML Requirements

List of common Entities & their respective general requirements

Listed Entity

  • Copy of the companies listing on a known stock exchange i.e. print out from Reuters, NYSE, etc
  • A list of Directors

    • Subsidiary of a Listed Entity:

  • A certificate of Incorporation
  • A certified list of Directors
  • Documentation showing the beneficial ownership of the company - this can be by way of a copy of the members register, certified structure chart with shareholding percentages, up to date annual report/quarterly report along with confirmation from In-House Council
  • Copy of the companies listing on a known stock exchange i.e. print out from Reuters, NYSE, etc

    • Regulated Entity

  • Confirmation from a regulated body as proof of regulation

    • Subsidiary of a Regulated Entity:

  • A certificate of Incorporation
  • A certified list of Directors
  • Documentation showing the beneficial ownership of the company - this can be by way of a copy of the members register, certified structure chart with shareholding percentages, up to date annual report/quarterly report along with confirmation from In-House Council
  • Confirmation from a regulated body as proof of regulation

    • Limited Company / Designated Activity Company (DAC) / Standard company:

  • A certificate of Incorporation/Proof of Registration
  • A certified list of Directors
  • A certified copy of photographic proof of ID & A certified copy of residential proof of address of a director. (1 required for legal/accountancy firms, 2 for financial firms
  • Documentation showing the beneficial & controlling ownership of the company - this can be by way of a copy of the members register, certified structure chart with shareholding percentages, up to date annual report/quarterly report along with confirmation from In-House Council
  • A certified copy of photographic proof of ID & A certified copy of residential proof of address of each individual who owns 25% or more in the company

    • Partnership:

  • A certificate of Incorporation
  • A certified list of Partners
  • A certified copy of photographic proof of ID & A certified copy of residential proof of address of a partner. (1 required for legal/accountancy firms, all partners required for financial firms
  • Documentation showing the beneficial & controlling ownership of the partnership - this can be by way of a copy of the members register, certified structure chart with shareholding percentages, up to date annual report/quarterly report along with confirmation from In-House Council
  • A certified copy of photographic proof of ID & A certified copy of residential proof of address of each individual who owns 25% or more in the company

    • Private Clients / Individuals:

  • A certified copy of photographic proof of Identification , i.e. Drivers License, Passport.
  • A certified copy of residential proof of address , i.e. utility bill, Revenue letter. (must be dated within 6 months for financial institutions & 12 months for legal institutions)
